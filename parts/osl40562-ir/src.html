Optosupply OSL40562-IR

The OSL40562-IR is a 0.56 inch 4-digit 7-segment LED display. It has a common anode.

Forward current

IF = (V − VF − VOL) / R

V = 3.3 V, R = 180 Ω

IF = (3.3 V − [1.8 – 2.3 V] − [0 – 0.4 V]) / 180 Ω ≈ 3 – 8 mA

IDIG.max = IF.max × 8 = 64 mA
