ROHM SLI-343x8

The SLI-343x8 is a series of 3 mm LEDs. It has a rated forward current of 20 mA.

Forward current

IF = (V − VF − VOL) / R

V = 3.3 V, R = 180 Ω

IF = (3.3 V − [1.8 – 2.2 V] − [0 – 0.4 V]) / 180 Ω ≈ 4 – 8 mA

V = 3.3 V, R = 330 Ω

IF = (3.3 V − [1.8 – 2.2 V] − [0 – 0.4 V]) / 330 Ω ≈ 2 – 5 mA
